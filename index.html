BLACKPINK

A South Korean girl group that debuted on 8 August 2016.

Kim Jisoo

Singer

Jisoo was born in South Korea on 3 January 1995. In 2011, she joined YG Entertainment as a trainee. Later she became a member of Blackpink. In 2015, she made her first appearance as an actress.

Kim Jennie

Rapper & Singer

Jennie was born on January 16, 1996 in Cheongdam-dong, Gyeonggi, South Korea. She moved to New Zealand to live at age 8 and attended Waikowhai Elementary School as an exchange student. In August 2010, she became a trainee of YG Entertainment.
